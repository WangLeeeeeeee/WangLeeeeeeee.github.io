Biography

Li Wang is a graduate student of automation science and electrical engineering at Beihang University. His research interests include robotics and control theory, now he works at IR&MCT lab under professor Weihai Chen. Now, his research area is cable-driven upper limb rehabilitation robot, aiming at helping stroke patients regain their motor function by using lightweigh cable-driven exoskeleton, his major task is modeling of robots, designing hardware and control algorithm.

Cable-Driven Upper-Extremity Exoskeleton Rehabilitation Robot

  • Supported by National Natural Science Foundation of China, aiming at rehabilitating stroke patients byusing cable-driven exoskeleton.
